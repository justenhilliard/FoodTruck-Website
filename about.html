Our Story

Meet Justen & The Crew

It all started with a simple belief: chicken should never be boring. Founded by Justen, a lifelong foodie with a passion for the perfect crunch, Justen's Yumtastic Chicken was born out of countless late-night recipe trials and a dream to bring gourmet comfort food straight to the streets.

We spent months perfecting our signature batter and secret spice blends. We didn't want to make just ordinary fried chicken—we wanted to create an experience that leaves you saying "Wow, that is truly yumtastic!" with every single bite.
